[Metamolecular](http://metamolecular.com) continues to compile its [catalog of free chemistry databases and Web services](http://zusammen.metamolecular.com/2009/03/09/sixty-four-free-chemistry-databases-serialized). The newest entry [covers SIDER](http://zusammen.metamolecular.com/2009/05/22/sixty-four-free-chemistry-databases-part-13-exploring-know-drug-side-effects-with-sider), a remarkable tool that lets you explore the relationships between marketed drugs and their side effects. 4.  [Electronic Encyclopedia of Reagents for Organic Synthesis](http://zusammen.metamolecular.com/2009/03/19/sixty-four-free-chemistry-databases-part-4-encyclopedia-of-reagents-for-organic-synthesis)
5.  [CrystalEye](http://zusammen.metamolecular.com/2009/03/25/sixty-four-free-chemistry-databases-part-5-crystaleye)
6.  [Common Chemistry](http://zusammen.metamolecular.com/2009/03/31/sixty-four-free-chemistry-databases-part-6-common-chemistry-from-chemical-abstracts-service)

Scrum in Under Ten Minutes

By Richard L. Apodaca

2010-09-12T00:00:00.000Z

Scrum is an approach to project management that emphasizes short iterative cycles. Scrum and Scrum-like approaches are used a lot in software development, particularly when combined with other Agile methodologies, but can be applied to any project. There's a lot of information available about Scrum - almost too much. This short video by Hamid Shojaee may be helpful in understanding the core ideas.
